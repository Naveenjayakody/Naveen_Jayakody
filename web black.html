About Us

Professional • Cinematic • Artistic

NJ Wave Studio is a creative media house based in Galagedara, Nikaweratiya, Sri Lanka. We specialize in crafting cinematic wedding films, evocative photography, and original music productions. Our approach blends storytelling with technical craft — from pre-production planning to color grading and audio mastering.

Our founder, Naveen, brings years of experience in film and audio production and a passion for capturing authentic emotion. We collaborate closely with couples, artists, and brands to produce polished, meaningful work that resonates.

Mission: To create media that moves people — visually and sonically. Whether it’s a wedding day, a music video, or a studio session, we aim for cinematic clarity, emotional truth, and a refined aesthetic.
